    Raw Data: All Factors & Metrics

    Complete dataset showing all 13 investment factors collected from Census API and market analysis

    📊 Demographic Factors

    • Total Population - MSA resident count
    • Pop Growth - Year-over-year population change (%)
    • Employment Rate - % of labor force employed
    • Median Household Income - Average annual income ($)
    • Income Growth - Year-over-year income change (%)

    🏠 Real Estate Factors

    • Median Gross Rent - Average monthly rent ($)
    • Median House Value - Average property value ($)
    • Rent to Income Ratio - Monthly rent / monthly income (%)
    • Vacancy Rate - Empty units as % of total
    • Implied Value - Annual rent × cap rate

    💼 Investment Metrics

    • Cap Spread - Hotel Cap Rate - Multifamily Cap Rate (%)
    • Tax Differential - Hotel tax rate - Multifamily tax rate (%)
    • HERS Index Score - Energy efficiency (lower = more efficient)
